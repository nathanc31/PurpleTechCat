About Nathan

This is me — the person behind PurpleTechCat.

My Biography

I’m Nathan, an IT student from the L25 area who enjoys helping people feel confident with technology. I’m currently building my own home IT lab, working with virtual machines, Linux systems and real troubleshooting to grow my skills since February 2026.

Before this, I spent two years as a Volunteer IT Officer at Deaf Active, where I refurbished my Digital Hub page (WordPress), supported Google Workspace and Apps Script automation, delivered IT sessions, and carried out Windows 10–11 migrations with safe file backups. I received two achievement awards for my contribution.

PurpleTechCat brings together my IT skills, accessibility experience and community background to offer free, friendly and local tech support for schools, charities and families in the L25 area.
